Available Expeditions


EVENT: Ice Age
TIME: 2.4 Million Years Ago
GAME: Wooly Mammoth
3/5 Slots
About The Observation: Dying at the end of the Pleistocene period 10,000 years ago, the Wooly Mammoth is a predecessor of the now-known asian and african elephants. Witness the size of these creatures and experience basking in their presence along the cold world.


EVENT: Cretaceous Period
TIME: 82 Million Years Ago
GAME: Deinosuchus
7/8 Slots
About The Hunt: With all of its kind dying during the great extinction event, the Deinosuchus were gigantic relatives of the alligators and crocodiles, oversizing even the great Tyrannosaurus Rex. In this expedition, face off against the monstrous Deinosuchus and claim your photo trophy!


EVENT: Early Pleistocene
TIME: 2 Million Years Ago
GAME: Tasmanian Tiger
3/6 Slots
About The Observation/Hunt: One of the largest carnivorous marsupials, these Australian native creatures had become extinct in 1936 with the death of the last captive creature passing. In this expedition, experience the unique creatures of the old world and observe or hunt these marsupials.
