Rules & Regulations

Rules

History is a fickle concept, and any minute changes can drastically affect the present, so we request that consumers:
  • Keep within the Metal Path provided by the company during the trip.
  • Do NOT interact with any living being in the past, whether it be leaves or the animals.
  • Only hunt the animals marked by the tour guides.

Risks


Please keep in mind that the experience is not regulated by the government, so any problems during or after the trip are to be held liable by the consumers. Examples of such are as follows:
  • The consumers dying on the trip.
  • Changes in the future caused by the travelers.
  • Contamination of the past.

Safety

To ensure that the trip goes as smoothly as possible, we ask that consumers read over the following safety procedures:
  • Before entering the Time Machine, check our oxygen masks and test communications device.
  • On the metal path, as we get closer from our hunt, or any animals in the vicinity, keep as quiet as possible to not draw any unwanted attention.
  • If consumers feel as if they do not want to continue, we will head back to the Time Machine and half of the payment will be returned.
