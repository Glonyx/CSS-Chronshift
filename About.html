About Chronoshift Expeditions
About The Company

Chronoshift was founded by Braddeus Ingram III in the year 2024 after obtaining the rights to the Time Travel Machine. He set his sights on creating an out-of-world experience for the people to witness the creatures of history that the current and future generations have not experienced, and to be able to hunt or observe them.

Our Technology

With the only existing Time Machines in the world, our state-of-the-art Time Machines are capable of going back in time to historical time periods in a timely fashion. There are only 10 time machines currently operating in our facilities with plans for additional devices in the future.

Tour Guides
